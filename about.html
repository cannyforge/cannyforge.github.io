About CannyForge

CannyForge is an independent AI practice — publishing across agent systems, architecture, economics, and emerging applications. Written by a builder, for practitioners, executives, and investors shaping what comes next.

The lens is always a practitioner's: grounded in what actually happens when these systems run — not what the benchmarks say, not what the press releases claim. Every piece of writing is backed by real building.

Projects

CannyForge (open source) — self-healing AI agents. Catches mistakes in-context and corrects them without retraining or restarting the session. No human in the loop.
