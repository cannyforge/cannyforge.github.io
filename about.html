About CannyForge

CannyForge is where serious AI builders think and build. We ship open-source tools, write about what breaks in production, and track the economics reshaping how AI systems are designed and deployed.

The lens is always a practitioner's: grounded in what actually happens when these systems run — not what the benchmarks say, not what the press releases claim. Architecture, agent reliability, cost curves, and the failure modes that only show up at scale.

The Flagship Project

CannyForge (open source) — self-healing AI agents. It catches agent mistakes as they happen and injects targeted corrections in-context, keeping agents on track without interrupting the session. No restart, no lost progress, no human in the loop.
